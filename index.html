Rooted in the Land

We're Joshua and Sherri Kinder — a veterinarian and his wife building a small, sustainable homestead on the Kitsap Peninsula.

Guided by Joel Salatin's regenerative methods and Wendell Berry's agrarian philosophy, we raise animals the way nature intended: on pasture, with care, and without shortcuts.

Our Jersey cow Buttercup, Idaho Pasture Pigs Rooster and Turnip, and a flock of heritage layer hens share the land with a growing orchard and vegetable garden.

    Animals on grass with room to roam — season by season.

  • Stewardship
